Adding a marker to a map is simple, this essentially only requires the marker coordinates and adding a marker icon.

Here we use FontAwesome and the Awsome Markers plugin to easily create marker icons on the fly.

Markers allow a lot of actions, e.g. they may be made draggable, or may trigger certain actions when clicked.

Here we simply bind a popop with simple HTML text content to the marker that gets shown when the marker is clicked.
